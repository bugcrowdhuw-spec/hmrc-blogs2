## 1. Executive Summary

TaxPrep OpenCLAW Agent is an AI-powered tax assistant designed specifically for UK short-term rental and property landlords. Unlike general accounting software, TaxPrep focuses exclusively on the unique tax complexities of Airbnb, Booking.com, and other holiday let income—including MTD compliance, furnished holiday let (FHL) rules, and property-specific deductions.

This pricing scheme positions TaxPrep competitively against both general accounting platforms (Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent) and specialist property tools, while emphasising the AI automation value that differentiates our offering.
