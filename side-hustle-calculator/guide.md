# Side Hustle Tax Calculator - Usage Guide

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Track Your Income
1. Open `income_tracker.csv` in Excel or Google Sheets
2. Add each payment from your side hustles
3. Include: Uber, Deliveroo, Fiverr, Upwork, tutoring, etc.

### Step 2: Log Expenses
1. Open `expense_logger.csv`
2. Record all business costs
3. Keep receipts for everything

### Step 3: Check Allowance
1. Open `allowance_checker.csv`
2. Enter your totals
3. See if trading allowance or expenses better

### Step 4: Estimate Quarterly Tax
1. Use `quarterly_estimator.csv` for payments on account
2. Due dates: 31 Jul, 31 Oct, 31 Jan, 30 Apr

### Step 5: Annual Summary
1. Use `annual_calculator.csv` for year-end
2. Calculate tax and NIC
3. File by 31 January

## Common Mistakes

1. Not tracking all income
2. Forgetting platform fees
3. Mixing personal and business expenses
4. Missing quarterly payments
5. Not keeping receipts
6. Claiming allowance AND expenses (can't do both)
