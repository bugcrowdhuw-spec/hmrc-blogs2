## Quick Start

1. Open `income_tracker.csv` in Excel or Google Sheets
2. Add your income sources (Uber, Deliveroo, Fiverr, etc.)
3. Log expenses in `expense_logger.csv`
4. Check quarterly payments in `quarterly_estimator.csv`
5. Use `allowance_checker.csv` to see if you owe tax
6. Review annual totals in `annual_calculator.csv`

---

## Key Tax Facts

- **Trading Allowance:** First £1,000 tax-free
- **Class 2 NIC:** £12.50/week if profits over £6,725
- **Income Tax:** 20% basic, 40% higher
- **Quarterly Payments:** Due 31 Jul, 31 Oct, 31 Jan, 30 Apr
