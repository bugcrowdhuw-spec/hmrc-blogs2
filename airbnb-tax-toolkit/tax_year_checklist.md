# Tax Year-End Checklist for Airbnb Hosts

**Tax Year 2025/26**  
**Deadline: 5 April 2026**

---

## January-March Checklist

- [ ] Reconcile all platform payments (Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo)
- [ ] Collect all expense receipts
- [ ] Check for missing income entries
- [ ] Verify expense categories are correct
- [ ] Calculate total rental income
- [ ] Calculate total allowable expenses
- [ ] Review capital improvements for CGT
- [ ] Check MTD compliance status
- [ ] Prepare for Self Assessment (if required)
- [ ] Deadline: 31 January for online filing

---

## MTD Requirements (2025/26)

If your property income exceeds £50,000:
- [ ] Using MTD-compatible software
- [ ] Digital records maintained
- [ ] Quarterly reports submitted
- [ ] Final return filed by 31 January

---

## Records to Keep

- [ ] Income records (7 years)
- [ ] Expense receipts (7 years)
- [ ] Property deeds/valuation
- [ ] Bank statements
- [ ] Platform reports

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. Missing platform income - HMRC receives data from Airbnb/Booking.com
2. Incorrect expense categorization
3. Forgetting to claim capital allowances
4. Missing the 31 January deadline
5. Not separating personal and rental expenses
