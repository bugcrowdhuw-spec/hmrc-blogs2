## Getting Started

### 1. Income Tracker
- Enter your property details in the Properties sheet
- Record each booking/payment in the Income sheet
- The Summary sheet auto-calculates totals

### 2. Expenses Calculator
- Enter expenses in the Expenses sheet
- Categories auto-match to allowable deductions
- Running total shows deductible amount

### 3. Capital Gains Estimator
- Enter purchase price and improvements
- Enter current estimated value
- Calculates estimated CGT liability

### 4. Tax Year Checklist
- Review at tax year end (5 April)
- Complete each task before Self Assessment deadline

---

## Quick Start

1. Open `income_tracker.csv` in Excel or Google Sheets
2. Add your properties
3. Start recording income
4. Use `expenses_calculator.csv` for expenses
5. Check `tax_year_checklist.md` before 5 April
