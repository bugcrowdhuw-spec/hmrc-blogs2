## Quick Start

1. Track income in `income_tracker.csv`
2. Check tax in `tax_checker.csv`
3. Under £12,570 = no income tax

---

## Key Facts

- **Personal Allowance:** £12,570
- **Interest:** £500 tax-free
- Part-time work usually tax-free
