# Product Proposal: Digital Products for UK Property Professionals

## Market Context

**Key trends driving demand (2026):**
- MTD for Income Tax Self-Assessment mandatory from 6 April 2026 for landlords/property income over £50k
- HMRC data-sharing agreements with Airbnb/Booking.com since Jan 2024 — hosts under scrutiny
- Section 24 mortgage interest relief changes continue affecting BTL profitability
- Many landlords still using spreadsheets, unprepared for digital compliance

**Target audience pain points:**
- Landlords overwhelmed by MTD requirements, seeking simple solutions
- Airbnb hosts confused about tax treatment (trading vs property income, CGT, allowable expenses)
- Property managers need efficient tools for multiple client portfolios
- Accountants seek automation for high-volume property clients

---

## Product Ideas

### Product 1: MTD-Ready Property Tax Dashboard

**What it is:** A browser-based dashboard that connects to bank feeds, categorises property income/expenses automatically, and generates quarterly MTD-compatible reports for HMRC submission.

**Value proposition:** Eliminates spreadsheet anxiety. Landlords get MTD compliance without learning new software.

**Target:** UK landlords with 1-10 rental properties, especially those nervous about MTD.

**Pricing:**
- £9.99/month (individual landlords)
- £29.99/month (portfolio up to 10 properties)

**Delivery:** SaaS (subscription), self-service onboarding, email support.

---

### Product 2: Airbnb Tax Compliance Toolkit

**What it is:** A downloadable toolkit (spreadsheet + video guide + PDF checklist) covering: annual income summary, allowable expenses calculator, capital gains estimator, and tax year-end checklist specifically for UK short-term lets.

**Value proposition:** Clear, actionable guidance for a confusing niche. Saves hours of research.

**Target:** UK Airbnb/VRBO/Booking.com hosts, especially those with mixed property portfolios (short-term + long-term).

**Pricing:**
- £47 one-time purchase (individual host)
- £97 one-time purchase (host with 3+ properties)

**Delivery:** Instant download (Google Sheets + PDF guide), plus 30-min video walkthrough.

---

### Product 3: Property Manager's Multi-Client Accounting Template

**What it is:** A sophisticated Google Sheets/Excel template designed for property managers handling 10-50 landlord clients. Includes client segregation, quarterly reporting per client, expense categorisation, and profit summaries.

**Value proposition:** Affordable alternative to expensive property management software. No subscription, works offline.

**Target:** Small property management firms, virtual assistants managing landlord portfolios, accountants with property client niche.

**Pricing:**
- £149 one-time (single user)
- £349 (team licence up to 5 users)

**Delivery:** Downloadable spreadsheet with 60-min video training + PDF manual.

---

## Recommendation

**Recommended: Product 2 — Airbnb Tax Compliance Toolkit**

**Justification:**
1. **Lowest build cost** — spreadsheet + content, no software development
2. **Immediate market need** — 2026 tax changes for short-term lets confusion, HMRC data-sharing already live
3. **Clear differentiation** — existing tools (Landlord Vision, RentalBux) focus on general landlords, not Airbnb-specific
4. **Scalable** — digital download has 80%+ margin, can add premium tiers (1:1 consultation)
5. **Content marketing hook** — easy to drive traffic via SEO blog content about Airbnb tax

**Risk note:** MTD dashboard (Product 1) has larger market but higher competition from established players. Toolkit approach is faster to launch and validate.
