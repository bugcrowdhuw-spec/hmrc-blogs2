# Investment Income Tracker - Usage Guide

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Set Up Your Accounts
1. Open `dividend_tracker.csv` in Excel or Google Sheets
2. Create a copy for your own use
3. Add your investments under "Symbol/Name"

### Step 2: Track Dividends
- Record each dividend payment as received
- Note the account type (ISA = tax-free, General = taxable)
- For non-GBP dividends, convert using the exchange rate on the payment date

### Step 3: Track Interest
- Use `interest_tracker.csv` for savings interest
- Include interest from all accounts except ISAs

### Step 4: Track Capital Gains
- Use `cgt_calculator.csv` for any asset sales
- Include shares, crypto, and other investments
- Personal items sold at a loss don't need to be tracked for CGT

### Step 5: Annual Summary
- At year-end, transfer totals to `annual_summary.csv`
- This goes into your Self Assessment

### Step 6: Estimate Tax
- Use `tax_estimator.csv` to calculate what you may owe
- Check `dividend_allowance.csv` for the £500 allowance

## Common Mistakes

1. Forgetting ISAs are tax-free
2. Missing dividend payments
3. Not converting foreign currency
4. Forgetting crypto CGT
5. Missing the CGT annual exemption
