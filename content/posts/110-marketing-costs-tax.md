# Marketing Costs for Airbnb Properties

**Marketing costs are fully deductible.**

## Allowable Costs

### Listing Fees
- Professional photos
- Airbnb promotion
- Platform upgrades

### Other Marketing
- Website costs
- Brochures
- Social media promotion

**Market and deduct.**
