## Year-End Property Income Preparation Guide

The tax year ends 5th April. Here's how to prepare:

### By End of March
1. **Reconcile all income** - Add up all rental income from all sources
2. **Reconcile all expenses** - Ensure every allowable expense is captured
3. **Generate reports** - Create your property income reports
4. **Check for errors** - Verify all numbers match

### Key Documents Needed
- Platform statements from all channels
- Bank statements
- Expense receipts and invoices
- Agent statements

### What to Do If You Find Errors
- Amend before filing
- Keep notes on corrections
- Document your reasoning

### Planning for Next Year
- Consider software to track automatically
- Set up monthly reconciliation
- Keep digital records
