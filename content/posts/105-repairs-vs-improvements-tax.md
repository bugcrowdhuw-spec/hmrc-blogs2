# Repairs vs Improvements: Tax Treatment

**This distinction matters hugely for tax.**

## Repairs — Deductible

### Examples
- Fixing leaks
- Replacing broken items
- Redecoration between lets

## Improvements — Capital

### Examples
- New bathroom
- Extension
- New boiler

### Tax Treatment
- Add to cost base
- Reduce CGT when sold

## Why It Matters

Wrong classification = penalties.
