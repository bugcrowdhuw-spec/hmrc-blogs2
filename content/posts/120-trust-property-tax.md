# Property Held in Trust: Tax Treatment

**Trusts have different tax treatment.**

## Income Tax

### Beneficiary
- Taxed on distribution
- Trust pays or retains

## Capital Gains

### Treatment
- Trust CGT rate
- 24% for residential

## Inheritance Tax

### Trust Property
- Usually outside trust
- 10 year charges
