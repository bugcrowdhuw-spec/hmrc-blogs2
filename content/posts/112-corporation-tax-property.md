# Corporation Tax for Property Companies

**Using a company affects tax treatment.**

## Corporation Tax Rates

### Current Rate
- 25% on profits over £250k
- Small profits rate available

## Property Income in Companies

### Treatment
- Taxed as company income
- Dividend distribution
- Different to personal

## Pros and Cons

### Advantages
- Lower top-rate tax
- Flexible extraction

### Disadvantages
- More complexity
- SDLT higher
