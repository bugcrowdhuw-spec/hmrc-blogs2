# Software Subscriptions for Property Management

**Software is a valid business expense.**

## Allowable Subscriptions

### Property Software
- Booking management
- Accounting software
- HMRC reporting tools

### What Counts
- Monthly/annual fees
- Per-property licenses

## Deducting Software

Add to your business expenses.
