# Accountant Fees for Property Income

**Accountant fees are usually deductible.**

## Allowable Costs

### Tax Return Preparation
- Filing your return
- Advice on tax planning

### Investigation Support
- HMRC correspondence
- Representation

## What NOT to Claim

Fees for non-tax work.
