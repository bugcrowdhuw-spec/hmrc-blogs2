# HMRC Reporting Obligations for Letting Agents

**Letting agents must register and keep records.**

## Agent Registration

If you handle client money - rent collected for landlords - you must register with HMRC as an agent.

## Record Keeping

Keep per landlord: rent collected, expenses paid, tax deducted. Minimum six years.

## Tax Withholding

For non-resident landlords: deduct 20% tax from rent and pay quarterly.
