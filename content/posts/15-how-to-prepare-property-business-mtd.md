# How to Prepare Your Property Business for MTD

**MTD compliance isn't optional for much longer. Here's how to prepare your property business — before deadlines catch you out.**

## Why Prepare Now

### The MTD Timeline

MTD is expanding:

- **Already mandatory** — For VAT-registered businesses
- **Rolling out** — To all landlords gradually
- **Coming soon** — Expect universal participation

**Current position:** If you're not in MTD, you will be soon. Preparation now prevents scramble later.

### Don't Wait

Waiting creates risks:

- **Limited software options** — Last-minute choices
- **Rushed implementation** — More errors
- **Tight deadlines** — Missed penalties
- **Poor record-keeping** — Audit exposure

Early adoption provides benefits:

- **Better cash flow** — Spread tax payments
- **Cleaner records** — Year-round accuracy
- **Reduced stress** — No annual panic
- **Real-time visibility** — Know your position

## Assessment Phase

### Step 1: Audit Your Current Position

Before preparing, understand where you are:

**Property Portfolio:**
- Number of properties
- Types (residential, holiday let, HMO)
- Ownership structure (personal, company, trust)
- Locations (UK only or international)

**Income Sources:**
- Platforms used (Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo)
- Direct bookings
- Long-term rentals
- Other income

**Current Records:**
- Paper or digital?
- Spreadsheet or software?
- Organized or scattered?

### Step 2: Calculate Your Exposure

Calculate for potential MTD:

| Factor | Questions |
|--------|-----------|
| Turnover | Total rental income past 12 months |
| VAT status | Over £85,000 threshold? |
| Complexity | Multiple properties? Different types? |
| Current workflow | Manual or automated? |

## Implementation Steps

### Phase 1: Digital Records

1. **Scan paper documents** — Convert to digital
2. **Set up folder structure** — Organized system
3. **Choose storage** — Cloud vs local
4. **Establish naming conventions** — Consistent approach

### Phase 2: Software Setup

1. **Create account** — Register software
2. **Configure properties** — Add all rentals
3. **Connect platforms** — Link booking accounts
4. **Set up bank feeds** — Automatic imports
5. **Configure categories** — Expense coding

### Phase 3: Migration

1. **Export current data** — From spreadsheets
2. **Clean data** — Fix errors, duplicates
3. **Import to new software** — Verify accuracy
4. **Reconcile** — Match to bank statements
5. **Generate test reports** — Verify outputs

### Phase 4: Ongoing Process

1. **Record continuously** — Daily/weekly, not batch
2. **Review monthly** — Check imported data
3. **File quarterly** — Meet deadlines
4. **Report annually** — Self Assessment support

## Workflow Optimisation

### Monthly Tasks

- **Review bank feed** — Check new transactions
- **Categorise expenses** — Ensure proper coding
- **Reconcile platform data** — Match to bank
- **File quarterly** — If MTD deadline

### Quarterly Tasks

- **Review submission** — Accuracy check
- **Calculate tax estimate** — Budget for payment
- **Update records** — Fix any issues
- **Plan next quarter** — Anticipate changes

### Annual Tasks

- **Year-end reconciliation** — Complete profit calculation
- **Prepare Self Assessment** — Compile return
- **Gather supporting documents** — Receipts, statements
- **Review tax position** — Planning for next year

## FAQ

### How long does MTD implementation take?

Proper implementation: 2-4 weeks for setup, ongoing adjustment. Don't wait until deadline approaches.

### What if my current records are a mess?

Start now. Clean up historical records as you go. Focus on going forward with clean processes.

### Can I do MTD myself or do I need an accountant?

You can do it yourself with good software. Accountants provide value for complex situations and tax planning.

### What is the biggest MTD mistake?

Waiting until the last minute. Start preparation now to avoid penalties and stress.
