<div class="checklist-section">
<h2><span class="section-num">1</span> Know Your Numbers</h2>

<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Calculate your total <strong>gross</strong> property income (before platform fees — not your payout)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Add income from all platforms: Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo, direct bookings</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Confirm whether you're above the £50,000 MTD threshold for April 2026</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">If below £50,000 — check when the £30,000 threshold applies (April 2027)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Check if you're approaching the £90,000 VAT threshold<span class="check-note">Airbnb's simplified pricing (15% fee) inflates your gross turnover. <a href="/posts/airbnb-simplified-pricing-vat-threshold-uk/">See our VAT threshold guide →</a></span></div></div>
</div>

<div class="checklist-section">
<h2><span class="section-num">2</span> Digital Records</h2>

<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text"><strong>Stop relying on paper records or memory</strong> — MTD requires digital records</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Create a digital record of every income transaction (date, amount, source)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Create a digital record of every expense (date, amount, category)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Set up a system to keep records for at least 5 years after the 31 January deadline</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Move off paper-only or memory-only tracking — spreadsheets alone are <strong>not MTD-compliant</strong></div></div>
</div>

<div class="checklist-section">
<h2><span class="section-num">3</span> Choose Your Software</h2>

<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Decide between <strong>bridging software</strong> (keeps your spreadsheets) or <strong>automated reporting</strong> (no spreadsheets) <span class="check-note"><a href="/posts/bridging-software-vs-automated-reporting-mtd/">Compare both approaches →</a></span></div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">If bridging: confirm it links to HMRC's MTD API (Absolute Accounting, Able, TaxCalc, etc.)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">If automated: connect your platform accounts</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Confirm your software handles <strong>gross income</strong> — not just net payouts</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Test a quarterly submission before your first real deadline</div></div>

<div class="checklist-section">
<h2><span class="section-num">4</span> Quarterly Submissions</h2>

<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Understand the quarterly cycle: 4 updates per year + end-of-period statement + final declaration</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Mark your calendar: first quarterly deadline is one month after the end of each quarter</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Q1: 6 Apr – 5 Jul → submit by 5 Aug</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Q2: 6 Jul – 5 Oct → submit by 5 Nov</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Q3: 6 Oct – 5 Jan → submit by 5 Feb</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Q4: 6 Jan – 5 Apr → submit by 5 May</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Know that late quarterly submissions trigger the same penalties as late Self Assessment</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">You still file an annual Self Assessment (end-of-period statement + final declaration)</div></div>
</div>

<div class="checklist-section">
<h2><span class="section-num">5</span> Expense Categories</h2>

<p style="color:#64748b;margin-bottom:12px;">Make sure you're tracking all allowable expenses. Check off the ones you're already recording:</p>

<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Platform fees (Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo service fees)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Cleaning and turnover costs between guests</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Utilities (electricity, gas, water, broadband)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Insurance (buildings, contents, landlord)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Maintenance and repairs</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Council tax (if you pay it)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Supplies (toiletries, linen, welcome packs)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Professional fees (accountant, tax adviser)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Advertising and listing fees</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Replacement of domestic items (furnishings — not initial furnishing)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Mortgage interest (tax credit at 20% for residential property)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Travel — only for business purposes (property visits, collecting supplies)</div></div>
</div>

<div class="checklist-section">
<h2><span class="section-num">6</span> Multi-Platform Reconciliation</h2>

<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text"><strong>Combine income from all platforms</strong> into one digital record</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Don't double-count: if a property is on Airbnb and Booking.com, track each booking separately</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Reconcile platform payouts against your bank statements monthly</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Keep platform export files (CSVs) as supporting evidence</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Track direct bookings separately — no platform data to fall back on</div></div>
</div>

<div class="checklist-section">
<h2><span class="section-num">7</span> VAT Position</h2>

<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Calculate your combined <strong>gross</strong> turnover from all sources</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">If approaching £70,000: start tracking monthly</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Know that you must register for VAT within 30 days of hitting £90,000</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Understand late registration = backdated VAT + penalties + interest</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Know the deregistration threshold: £88,000 (if turnover drops)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Consider whether voluntary VAT registration makes sense for your situation</div></div>
</div>

<div class="checklist-section">
<h2><span class="section-num">8</span> Before Your First Submission</h2>

<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Register for MTD-compatible software and link it to your HMRC account</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Import or enter all income and expenses for the current tax year</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Run a test quarterly submission (most software allows a dry run)</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Confirm your National Insurance number and UTR are linked correctly</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">If you have an accountant: share software access so they can review before submission</div></div>
<div class="check-item"><div class="check-box">✓</div><div class="check-text">Set up automated data imports where possible — reduce manual entry errors</div></div>
</div>
