# Private Residence Relief for Property

**Main home relief reduces CGT significantly.**

## Qualifying

### Requirements
- Owned property
- Used as only/main home
- During ownership period

## Calculation

### Final Period
- Last 9 months exempt
- Even if not living there
- If no other residence
