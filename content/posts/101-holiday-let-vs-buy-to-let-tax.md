# Holiday Let vs Standard Buy-to-Let: Tax Differences

**Different tax rules apply. Here's what changes.**

## Income Tax Treatment

### Holiday Let
- Treated as trade
- More flexible losses
- Can offset other income

### Standard Buy-to-Let
- Property income
- Loss restrictions
- Basic treatment

## Capital Allowances

### Holiday Let
- Annual investment allowance
- Furniture, equipment
- Full deductions

### Standard Buy-to-Let
- Reduced allowances
- Limited relief

## Which Is Better?

Depends on your situation:

- Want flexibility? Holiday let
- Want simplicity? Buy-to-let
