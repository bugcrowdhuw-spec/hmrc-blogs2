# Capital Gains Tax on Property Sales

**Selling property triggers CGT.**

## CGT Rates

### Residential Property
- 18% or 28%
- Depends on income

## Allowable Deductions

- Purchase price
- Improvement costs
- Agent/legal fees

## Reliefs

- Private Residence Relief
- Letting Relief (limited)
- Holdover

---

**Plan for CGT on sale.**
