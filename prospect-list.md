## Suggested Outreach Approach

### For Accountants:
1. Use email templates from `accountant-outreach-emails.md`
2. Start with the 8 "high priority" firms above
3. LinkedIn connect + personalised message as backup
4. Follow up after 5-7 days if no response

### For Property Managers:
1. Position as: "Your clients need HMRC reports. We automate that. Want to offer it as a value-add?"
2. Offer: Free/discounted accounts for the management company to test
3. Angle: "Save your clients 5+ hours per quarter on tax reporting"
4. Ask: Can you add us to your recommended tools/resources page?

### Quick Wins to Target First:
- Post helpful answers on the Airbnb Community forum thread about tax software
- Comment on LinkedIn posts from DNS/CBS/Major/UHY about holiday let tax
- Join Property118 and LandlordZone — answer tax questions genuinely
