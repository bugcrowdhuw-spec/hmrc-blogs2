# Utility Expenses for Airbnb Properties

**Utilities are partially deductible. Here's how it works.**

## Allowable Utility Costs

### When Property is Let
- Gas, electric, water
- Internet
- TV license

### Calculation Method
- Pro-rata by days let
- Or flat percentage

### What NOT to Claim
- When property empty
- Personal use portion
