# Letting Agent Fees Tax Treatment

**Agent fees are fully deductible.**

## Allowable Fees

### Management Fees
- Monthly charge
- Tenancy setup
- Inventory

### Finding Fees
- Tenant Finder
- Referencing costs

## What You Deduct

Add to expenses on your return.

---

**Agent fees reduce your tax.**
