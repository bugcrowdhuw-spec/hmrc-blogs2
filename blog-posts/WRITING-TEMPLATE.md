1. **Hook** — strong pain point in first 100 words (confusion, time waste, compliance risk)
2. **The Problem** — multiple income sources, manual spreadsheets, reconciliation difficulty, incorrect reporting risk
3. **Regulations Explained** — HMRC rules, MTD requirements, simple and practical
4. **Step-by-Step Process** — numbered steps, actionable
5. **Agitate the Problem** — time wasted, scaling complexity, error/penalty risk, stress
6. **Solution (soft positioning)** — software as logical next step: automation, accuracy, time-saving. Educational, not salesy.
7. **Soft CTA** — "Learn more about simplifying your HMRC reporting" etc.
8. **FAQ Section** — 3–5 relevant questions

## FAQ

### "Do I need to declare this?"
Yes - if it's income from property, it needs to be declared to HMRC.

### "What expenses can I claim?"
Allowable expenses include repairs, insurance, utilities when let, agent fees, and professional costs.

### "What happens if I get it wrong?"
HMRC may charge penalties and interest. In serious cases, they may investigate.
