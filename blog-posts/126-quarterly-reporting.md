# Quarterly Reporting for Property Income

**MTD requires quarterly reports.**

## Quarters

### Dates
- Q1: Apr-Jun (due Jul)
- Q2: Jul-Sep (due Oct)
- Q3: Oct-Dec (due Jan)
- Q4: Jan-Mar (due May)

## What to Report

- Income
- Expenses
- Net profit

---

**Report quarterly.**
