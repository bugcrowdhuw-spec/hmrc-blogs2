# Insurance Costs You Can Claim for Airbnb

**Insurance is fully deductible. Here's what counts.**

## Allowable Insurance

### Buildings Insurance
- Landlord insurance
- Buildings and contents
- Liability cover

### Other Insurance
- Guest injury cover
- Loss of earnings
- Legal expenses

## What NOT to Claim

Premium allocated to personal use periods.
