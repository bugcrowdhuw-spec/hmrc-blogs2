# Vrbo Tax Reporting Guide for UK Hosts

**Vrbo (formerly HomeAway) is a major UK short-term rental platform. Here's what UK hosts need to know about reporting their Vrbo income to HMRC.**

## Understanding Vrbo Platform

### What Is Vrbo?

Vrbo (Vacation Rentals By Owners) is part of the Expedia Group:

- **Global reach** — One of the largest holiday rental platforms
- **UK presence** — Significant market share in UK holiday lets
- **Property types** — Houses, apartments, unique stays
- **Booking model** — Direct bookings with property owners

### How Vrbo Works

Vrbo operates differently from Airbnb:

- **No host liability programme** — Hosts arrange own insurance
- **Fee structure** — Listing fees + booking commissions
- **Payment timing** — Varies by booking configuration
- **Calendar sync** — Integrates with major channel managers

### Vrbo Data HMRC Receives

Under digital platform reporting rules, Vrbo reports to HMRC:

- **Total annual earnings** — Your year-end total
- **Property addresses** — All your UK listings
- **Booking volumes** — Nights booked per property
- **Your details** — Name, address, tax identification

## Accessing Your Vrbo Reports

### Finding Your Earnings Data

1. **Log into Vrbo** — via vrbo.co.uk
2. **Access Host Dashboard** — Your control panel
3. **Go to Finance** — Earnings and reports
4. **Select Date Range** — Choose tax year
5. **Download Report** — Export data for tax

### Key Reports to Download

**Annual Summary:**
- Total year earnings
- Number of bookings
- Average nightly rate
- Fee breakdown

**Property Breakdown:**
- Per-property earnings
- Booking frequency
- Revenue by property

**Transaction History:**
- Individual booking payments
- Fee deductions
- Payout dates

## Reporting Vrbo Income to HMRC

### Self Assessment Process

1. **Gather Vrbo data** — Download annual report
2. **Add to SA105** — Property income section
3. **Include all income** — Total earnings
4. **Deduct expenses** — Allowable costs
5. **Calculate profit** — Net taxable amount

### What to Include

All Vrbo income:

- **Booking payments** — Guest payments via platform
- **Cleaning fees** — Guest-paid cleaning
- **Security deposits** — Retained deposits
- **Direct payments** — Bank transfers

### Matching Platform Data

HMRC cross-checks your return:

- **Platform reports** — Vrbo sends data to HMRC
- **Your declaration** — What you report
- **Must match** — Or HMRC investigation triggers

**Critical:** If numbers differ, investigate immediately.

## FAQ

### Does Vrbo report to HMRC automatically?

Yes. Under OECD digital platform rules, Vrbo shares your earning data with HMRC automatically. Your Self Assessment must match.

### What if I don't declare all my Vrbo income?

Platform data is shared automatically. Not declaring triggers investigation, penalties, and interest charges. Always report accurately.

### Can I claim the £1,000 Property Income Allowance on Vrbo income?

Yes. If your total UK property income (including Vrbo) is under £1,000, you can claim this allowance.

### How do I handle Vrbo if I'm also on Airbnb?

Combine all platform income on your Self Assessment. Keep per-platform records for accuracy, report total income.
