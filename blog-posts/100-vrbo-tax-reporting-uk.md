# Vrbo Tax Reporting Guide for UK Hosts

**Vrbo is part of the Expedia family. Here's how to report your Vrbo income.**

## Vrbo Reporting

### What Vrbo Provides

- Annual earnings statement
- Property-by-property breakdown
- Fee breakdown

### Accessing Reports

1. Log into vrbo.co.uk
2. Go to "Host Dashboard"
3. Click "Finance"
4. Export your report

## Reporting to HMRC

### Include with Self Assessment

Add Vrbo income to:

- SA105 property pages
- Total property income

### Match Platform Data

Ensure consistency:

- Vrbo shows £X
- You declare £X
- No gaps
