# Inheritance Tax on Property

**Property counts in your estate.**

## IHT Threshold

### Nil-Rate Band
- £325,000
- £500,000 with residence

## Rates

### Above Threshold
- 40% on excess
- Reduced if donated

## Planning

- Lifetime gifts
- Potentially exempt
- Trust arrangements
