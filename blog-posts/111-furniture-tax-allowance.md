# Furniture Allowance for Furnished Lets

**You can get tax relief for furnished properties.**

## Types of Relief

### Wear and Tear Allowance
- 10% of rent (for furnished)
- Replacement of domestic items
- Now replaced with RDS

### Replacement of Domestic Items
- Actual cost replacement
- Like-for-like basis
- No improvement allowed

## What Qualifies

- Furniture
- White goods
- Linen, curtains
