# Mortgage Interest on Airbnb Properties

**Interest relief is limited. Here's the current rules.**

## Current Rules

### For Standard Lets
- 20% tax credit
- Not full deduction

### For Holiday Lets
- Different treatment
- Usually fully deductible

## What You Can Claim

### Holiday Let
- Interest as expense
- Full deduction possible

### Important
- Check your status
- Get advice if unsure
