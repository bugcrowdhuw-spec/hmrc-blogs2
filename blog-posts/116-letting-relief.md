# Letting Relief: What It Is and How It Works

**Letting relief reduces CGT on rental properties.**

## Current Rules

### What It Was
- Up to £40,000
- Private + letting use

### Current
- Very limited
- Only where you live

## Qualifying

### Must Have
- Occupied as main home
- Also let during ownership
