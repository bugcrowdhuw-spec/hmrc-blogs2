# VAT Calculator - Usage Guide

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Check Registration
1. Open `registration_checker.csv`
2. Enter your annual taxable supplies
3. See if you need to register for VAT

### Step 2: Calculate VAT
1. Use `vat_calculator.csv` for day-to-day calculations
2. Add or remove VAT from amounts
3. Use correct rate (standard 20%, reduced 5%, zero 0%)

### Step 3: Consider Flat Rate
1. Check `flat_rate_calculator.csv` if under £150k turnover
2. Find your sector percentage
3. Calculate simplified VAT

### Step 4: Plan Returns
1. Use `quarterly_estimator.csv` to plan payments
2. Deadlines: 7 May, 7 Aug, 7 Nov, 7 Feb

---

## Key VAT Rates

| Rate | Applies To |
|------|------------|
| 20% | Standard - most goods and services |
| 5% | Reduced - energy, children's car seats |
| 0% | Zero - food, books, children's clothing |

---

## Registration Rules

- **Must register:** Over £90,000 taxable supplies
- **Can register:** Under £90,000 (voluntary)
- **Deregister:** Under £88,000

---

## Common Mistakes

1. Not registering when crossing £90k
2. Using wrong VAT rate
3. Missing quarterly deadlines
4. Not accounting for reverse charge
5. Forgetting import VAT on goods from EU
