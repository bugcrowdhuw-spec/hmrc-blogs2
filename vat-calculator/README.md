## Key VAT Facts

- **Registration Threshold:** £90,000
- **Deregistration Threshold:** £88,000
- **Standard Rate:** 20%
- **Reduced Rate:** 5% (energy, children's car seats)
- **Zero Rate:** Food, books, children's clothing
- **Flat Rate:** 6.5%-16.5% depending on sector
