# Image Prompts for Product Images

## Use these prompts with AI image generators (Midjourney, DALL-E, etc) or give to a designer

**Prompt:** Professional UK tax calculator spreadsheet on laptop screen, property symbols (house keys, pound signs), clean blue and white color scheme, modern minimalist design, British Pound currency symbol prominent

## Inheritance Tax Planning

## Airbnb Tax Toolkit

## Side Hustle Tax Calculator

## UK Tax Calendar

## General Product Image Style
- Clean minimalist design
- UK flag colors (red, white, blue) as accents  
- Spreadsheet/calculator on screen
- Professional, trustworthy financial look
- White or light gray background
- Text overlay with product name

## Recommended Image Size
- 1200 x 630 pixels (Facebook/Open Graph)
- 1280 x 720 pixels (YouTube thumbnail)
- 800 x 800 pixels (Instagram square)
- Include product name and price in image
