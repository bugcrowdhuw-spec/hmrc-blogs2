What Counts as Taxable Property Income in the UK

Every payment from renting property is taxable. Here’s what counts.

Core Rental Income

Every rent payment is taxable - monthly rent, per-night Airbnb rates, weekly lets. If a guest pays to stay, it’s income.

Additional Charges

  • Cleaning fees you keep
  • Late check-out fees
  • Cancellation fees you retain
  • Pet charges
  • Parking fees

Platform Payments

All platform payouts - Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO - are income. Include fees taken by platforms in your gross.

Direct Bookings

Money paid directly to your bank account is still rental income and must be declared.

What Doesn’t Count

  • Security deposits returned
  • Money passed to cleaners/vendors
  • Refunds given

Gross vs Net

HMRC cares about gross income before fees. Deduct expenses separately.
