SDLT Supplementary: Additional Properties

3% SDLT surcharge applies.

rules

When It Applies

  • Additional property
  • Already own a property
  • Not replacing main home

Rate

Surcharge

  • 3% on entire price
  • Applies above current rates

Exemptions

  • Replacing main residence
  • Inherited property

Account for SDLT in calculations.
