Cleaning Expenses You Can Claim on Airbnb

Cleaning is fully deductible. Here’s what counts.

Allowable Cleaning Costs

Between Guests

  • Professional cleaners
  • Per-booking cleaning
  • Supplies (detergents, etc.)

Regular Maintenance

  • Weekly cleans for lets
  • Deep cleaning periodically
  • Carpet/floor cleaning

What NOT to Claim

  • Personal cleaning
  • When property empty
  • Excessive frequency

Record Keeping

Keep invoices and receipts for all cleaning.

Claim your cleaning expenses.
