Booking.com Tax Reporting for UK Property Managers

If you use Booking.com, you need to report that income. Here’s how.

What Booking.com Reports

Platform Data

Booking.com reports to HMRC:

  • Total earnings per year
  • Property addresses
  • Booking details
  • Your host information

Getting Your Reports

From Booking.com extranet

  1. Log into extranet
  2. Go to “Finance”
  3. Click “-downloads”
  4. Select date range

What’s Included

  • Gross bookings
  • Platform fees
  • Net earnings
  • Cancellation adjustments

How to Report

Add to Self Assessment

Include Booking.com income with:

  • Airbnb income
  • All other platforms
  • Direct bookings

Match Platform Data

Ensure your numbers match:

  • Booking.com total = Your declared amount
  • Any differences need explaining

Track all platforms. Combine for accurate reporting.
