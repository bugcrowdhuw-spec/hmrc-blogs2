What Income Do Airbnb Hosts Need to Declare?

All Airbnb income must be declared. Here’s what counts.

The Rule

Every pound earned through Airbnb must be declared on Self Assessment.

What’s Included

  • All booking payments
  • Cleaning fees you keep
  • Pet fees
  • Any other charges

The £1,000 Allowance

You can claim the Property Income Allowance - up to £1,000 tax-free. But still declare the income.

Platform Reporting

Airbnb reports your earnings directly to HMRC. They will match against your return.
