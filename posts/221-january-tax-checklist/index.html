January Tax Checklist for Property Managers

As the new year begins, here’s your January checklist:

Records to Gather

  • All platform statements (Airbnb, Booking.com)
  • Bank statements showing rental income
  • Expense receipts from the previous year
  • Agent reports if you use a management company

Actions to Take

  • Reconcile all income for the past year
  • Ensure all expenses are captured
  • Generate your annual reports
  • Prepare for Self Assessment filing

Key Dates

  • 31 January - Self Assessment deadline
  • 31 July - Second payment on account

Don’t Forget

  • Set aside money for tax
  • Check your calculations
  • File even if you can’t pay (set up payment plan)
