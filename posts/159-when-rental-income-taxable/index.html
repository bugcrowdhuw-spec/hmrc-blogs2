When Does Rental Income Become Taxable in the UK?

Understanding tax timing is essential.

Tax Year Dates

UK tax year: 6th April to 5th April. All income in this period is taxed in that year.

When Income Is Taxable

Most use cash basis - taxed when received (hits bank). Simple and most common.

When to Register

Register for Self Assessment by 5th October in the tax year you first earn rental income.

Key Deadlines

  • 31 January - Return deadline
  • 31 January - Pay tax due
