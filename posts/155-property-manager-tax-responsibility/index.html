Do Property Managers Need to Report Client Income to HMRC?

Property managers have specific obligations for client income.

Your Tax Obligations

Your management fees are your business income - declare on Self Assessment or company return.

Client Monies

If you collect rent for landlords you may need to register as an agent.

Non-Resident Landlords

For non-UK resident landlords, deduct 20% tax from rent and pay quarterly to HMRC.
