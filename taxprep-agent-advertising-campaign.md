1. **Autonomous Client Support** — AI agent handles routine tax queries 24/7, freeing staff for complex work
2. **MTD Compliance** — Generates accurate MTD-compliant reports and reminders
3. **Scalability** — Serve unlimited clients without proportionally increasing staff time
4. **Passive Revenue** — Deliver tax advisory as a recurring service without overtime
5. **Competitive Differentiation** — Position firm as tech-forward, attract digitally-savvy clients

### Emotional Drivers

- **Fear:** Missing deadlines, HMRC penalties, client churn to more tech-savvy competitors
- **Frustration:** Repetitive client queries ("when is my deadline?", "what can I deduct?")
- **Ambition:** Want to scale practice without hiring expensive tax staff
- **Prestige:** Be known as the "tech-forward" accountant in the area

### Differentiators vs. Competitors

- Purpose-built for UK property tax (vs. generic AI chatbots)
- Runs on OpenCLAW framework — customisable, auditable, secure
- Commercial license includes ongoing updates and support
- Generates actual HMRC-ready outputs (not just guidance)
