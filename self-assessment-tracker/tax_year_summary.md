# UK Self Assessment Tax Year Summary 2025/26

## Key Deadlines

| Date | Deadline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| 31 Oct 2025 | Paper Return | 2024/25 tax return (if paper) |
| 31 Jul 2025 | First POA | Payment on account for 2025/26 |
| 31 Jan 2026 | Online Return | 2024/25 tax return (online) |
| 5 Apr 2026 | Tax Year End | End of 2025/26 |
| 6 Apr 2026 | New Tax Year | Start of 2026/27 |
| 31 Jul 2026 | Second POA | Second payment on account |

---

## Payment on Account

If you owe more than £1,000 in tax, you pay in two instalments:
- 50% by 31 July
- 50% by 31 January

---

## Penalties

| Late Filing | Penalty |
|--------------|---------|
| 1 day | £100 |
| 3 months | £10/day |
| 6 months | Greater of 5% tax or £300 |
| 12 months | Greater of 5% tax or £300 |

| Late Payment | Interest |
|--------------|----------|
| 30 days | 5% above Bank of England base rate |
| 6 months | Additional 5% |
| 12 months | Additional 5% |

## Getting Started Early

**12 weeks before deadline:**
- Gather all records
- Check your HMRC online account
- Register for Self Assessment if new

**8 weeks before deadline:**
- Calculate income from all sources
- Calculate allowable expenses
- Check if payments on account are needed

**4 weeks before deadline:**
- Complete your return
- Calculate tax owed
- Plan for payment

**1 week before deadline:**
- File your return
- Set up payment
- Keep proof of filing
