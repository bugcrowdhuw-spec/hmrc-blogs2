### Content Formula:
1. Hook (pain)
2. Education (HMRC rules + MTD impact)
3. Problem escalation (spreadsheets break at scale)
4. Soft sell (automated tools)
5. CTA (try platform)

### Ranking Tips:
- Target long-tail keywords
- 1,200–2,000 words per article
- Include FAQs
- Internal linking between all blogs
- Post 2–3 articles per week
- Goal: rank for multiple HMRC + Airbnb keywords in 3–6 months
